Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly's expense tracking features, we collect the information you give us directly, including your name, email address, and account password, as well as the expense records you create — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the Service: authenticating your account, displaying your expenses and summaries, and helping you understand your spending patterns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery services) to operate the Service. These providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at support@spendly.app.
